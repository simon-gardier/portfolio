title: "Human Activity Recognition - Machine Learning Competition"
description: "The goal of the competition was to predict human activity (Lying, walking stairs,...) based on data collected from various sensors (heart bpm, gyroscopes, magnetometers,...). The data is collected as a time series of 512 values, recorded during 5-seconds intervals and contains NaN values.
The participants submit their prediction on 3500 new examples, from which an accuracy score is computed on a subset of 10% (secret and fixed for everyone) of the testing set. Competition rank: 5th place (45 teams, 135 students)."
